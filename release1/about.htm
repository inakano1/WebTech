About Me

Fourth year undergraduate student at Babson College

Originally from Japan, grew up in the United States

Crypto enthusiast and investor

I was first aware of Bitcoin back in 2017 because my college roommate mentioned that he had purchased some. My initial reaction was immediate dismissal of Bitcoin, because I assumed that it was some sort of pyramid scheme, and that no digital money should be trusted. However at the end of 2020, I begun hearing more and more about the explosion in Bitcoin prices, and that early investors were making millions of dollars. This caught my attention. As I researched about cryptocurrencies, I realized that this is a highly rewarding investment and that this blockchain technology could be the future of money. So, I started investing immediately and learned more about the crypto industry over the last year. I believe that crypto prices will keep rising over the upcoming years, even though there will be crashes along the way. I hope to learn more about crypto and potentially be involved in the industry as a career in the future.
