What are Cryptocurrencies?

Cryptocurrency is a virtual, digitalized currency that uses blockchain technology to validate transactions and ownerships within the network. Blockchain technology allows the network to distribute an unchangeable ledger that is shared to and contributed by hundreds of thousands of mining computers, or nodes, around the world. This makes the network decentralized, meaning that there is no central authority that can alter and facilitate the transactions like banks or governments.
