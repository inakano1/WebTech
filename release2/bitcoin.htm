Bitcoin (BTC)


Bitcoin is the first, original cryptocurrency that was created in 2008 by an unknown person or group named Satoshi Nakamoto. It was created to serve as a decentralized online currency that is unhackable due to its revolutionary blockchain technology. Bitcoin has always remained at the top of the cryptocurrency rankings because it is the first of its kind with the most recognition and trust. The market capitalization of Bitcoin is roughly 1 trillion dollars as of October 2021, with one Bitcoin trading at around 57,000 dollars.
