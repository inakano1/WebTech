Ethereum (ETH)


Ethereum was founded by several co-founder, but the most notable individual from the group is Vitalik Buterin who published the Ethereum white paper. This coin not only serves as a currency, but can also be utilized for smart contracts. Ethereum has trailed behind Bitcoin in the cryptocurrency rankings, and is steadily gaining dominance in the crypto markets. The market capitalization of Ethereum is roughly 442 billion dollars as of October 2021, with one ETH trading at around 3,700 dollars.
