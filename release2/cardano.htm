Cardano (ADA)


Cardano was founded by Charles Hoskinson, who was actually one of the co-founders of Ethereum. This coin was one of first to successfully implement the proof-of-stake system, which allows the network to process transactions significantly faster and more efficient than proof-of-work systems like Bitcoin and Ethereum (ETH is currently transitioning to proof-of-stake system). Cardano has increased its market share in the cryptocurrency rankings over the past few months, and is ranked 4th just behing BNB. The market capitalization of Cardano is roughly 70 billion dollars as of October 2021, with one ADA trading at around 2.16 dollars.
