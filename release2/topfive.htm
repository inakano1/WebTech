Top Five Cryptocurrencies

There are hundreds of different cryptocurrencies that exist today, but there are only a handful of them which are widely recognized and have significant market capitalization. If you are considering investing in cryptocurrencies, the safest bet is to purchase only the major coins that have trust and actual utility, such as smart contract and DeFi capabilities, because those are more likely to continue grow without perishing along the way. (However it is important to note that cryptocurrencies in general are highly volatile and risky assets, so please do your own research and invest with caution)

With that being said, these are the top five major cryptocurrencies based on current market capitalization:
